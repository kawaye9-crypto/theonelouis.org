Behind The Blocks: What is This Pack?

[The Mission] This modpack is a hand-curated collection of over 250 mods designed to eliminate the repetitive grind and focus purely on creative progression. We blend complex automation with powerful magic to give you a truly unconventional Minecraft experience.

[Core Experience] The pack features custom recipes and progression paths using KubeJS to ensure mods like Mekanism and Botania work together seamlessly. Expect unique starting items, overhauled world generation, and challenging end-game bosses.

[Technical Info] Built on Minecraft 1.20.1 (Forge). We recommend allocating a minimum of 8GB of RAM for a smooth, lag-free experience.
